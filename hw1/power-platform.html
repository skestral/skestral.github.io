Microsoft Power Platform

A suite of low-code tools that empowers organizations to analyze data, build solutions, automate processes, and create AI-powered virtual agents.

The Power Platform is a family of five products from Microsoft that lets both professional developers and non-technical citizen developers build applications and automations with little to no code. It integrates natively with M365, Azure, and Dynamics 365.

  • Power Apps — build custom canvas and model-driven apps connected to any data source
  • Power Automate — automate repetitive workflows across cloud services and desktop applications
  • Power BI — create interactive data visualizations and business intelligence dashboards
  • Power Pages — design secure, external-facing websites backed by Microsoft Dataverse
  • Copilot Studio — build AI-powered chatbots and virtual agents without traditional coding
  • AI Builder — embed pre-built and custom AI models directly into apps and flows
  • Connectors — integrate with SharePoint, Salesforce, SQL Server, and hundreds of other services
