Governance & Microsoft Purview

Enterprise governance ensures that data is protected, compliant, and trustworthy. Microsoft Purview is Microsoft's unified suite for data governance, risk management, and compliance.

Microsoft Purview consolidates Azure Purview and the Microsoft 365 Compliance Center into one portal. It helps organizations meet regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI-DSS while keeping data classified, auditable, and protected across cloud and on-premises systems.

  • Information Protection — sensitivity labels encrypt and restrict documents and emails, persisting with the file wherever it travels
  • DLP Policies — prevent sensitive data from being shared outside the organization via Teams, Exchange, SharePoint, and endpoints
  • Compliance Manager — scores your compliance posture against GDPR, HIPAA, NIST, and hundreds of other frameworks with prioritized action plans
  • Insider Risk Management — uses machine learning to detect anomalous behavior such as data exfiltration, with privacy-preserving pseudonymization
  • Data Lifecycle Management — retention policies govern how long content is kept or deleted to satisfy legal holds and data minimization requirements
  • Audit & eDiscovery — a unified audit log and advanced eDiscovery workflow covering collection, review, and export for litigation or investigations
  • CoE Starter Kit — a Power Platform CoE toolkit that gives administrators visibility into every app, flow, and environment across the tenant

Source: Microsoft Purview – Microsoft Learn
